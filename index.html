About Me

Architect, Developer, DevOps Engineer with over 14 years experience including 6 years in the FinTech industry.

Expertise

Docker & Kubernetes

Many years experience using Docker for all aspects of engineering. From development environments running Ansible, MariaDB, Java, etc. Bamboo agents running on kubernetes, automated CI/CD pipelines and Docker registries on JFrog.

Cloud

Years of experience with Cloud technolgies including AWS. Getting the most our of the cloud is not about lifting a shifting existing VMs to the cloud, it's about leveraging Cloud technologies, especially serverless services like AWS S3, KMS and CloudFront.

Java

Over 10 years exerience with Java. Including TDD with JUnit and Mockito, Spring Core, Spring MVC, Spring Security, Spring Transactions, Spring AOP and many more.
